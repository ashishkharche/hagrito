## Description

Waiting to find out what you should feel about the Fall 2015 Season so far? We've got you covered!

[![](https://i.imgur.com/EPnQc1R.png)](http://traffic.libsyn.com/ranime/19_final_mixdown.mp3)

So you've started the Fall 2015 Anime Season, but you're just a little confused. You don't know how you feel about what could be your favorite or least favorite show. Your only option is to turn to the internet to find out how you should feel and what you should enjoy. Well, we're here to help! Our panel of "experts" will inform you of the feelings you have towards the best and worst anime of the season, so you can be sure you'll be the coolest kid at the anime club!
